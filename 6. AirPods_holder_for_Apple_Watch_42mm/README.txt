# Summary

Hi!

This is my first collaboration on the community!

Here I made a basic holder for the AirPods. This is attached to the stripe of my Apple Watch (42mm). I like the idea with the case from the AirPods, but I am not carrying it with me in the office, so, when I leave my desk for a short time, I put my AirPods on my holder, and voila! I don't need to put hem on my pocket or somewhere else, where for sure I am going to lose them.

So, here you have. I you like it, please like it/comment it.
